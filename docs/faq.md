# FAQ

## Is this a paper summarizer?

No. It can summarize, but its main purpose is deep reading: paragraph-level understanding, figure interpretation, and reusable study notes.

## Does it require Obsidian?

No. The output is Markdown, but it is optimized for Obsidian callouts, image links, and knowledge-base reading.

## Can I publish generated notes?

Be careful. Public examples should avoid copyrighted full-text translation unless the source license allows redistribution.

## Why keep a question log?

The question log turns confusion into reusable learning material. It also prevents repeated explanations from scattering across the note.
